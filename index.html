How To Read This Board
American odds
The sportsbook price. Negative means how much you risk to win $100. Positive means how much profit you win on a $100 bet.
Edge
NHL3.0 model win probability minus the market's fair probability after removing juice. Positive is better.
EV
Expected value on the listed odds. Positive EV means the model estimates the bet is profitable over time. Negative EV means pass.
Bet or Pass
Bet means the line cleared the current positive-value thresholds. Pass means the model does not see enough value at that price.
Confidence
Confidence runs Low, Medium-Low, Medium, Medium-High, and High. Higher confidence means the model sees a stronger gap between its number and the listed odds.
